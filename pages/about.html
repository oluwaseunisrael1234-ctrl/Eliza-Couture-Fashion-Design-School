About Eliza

Her Story

The Designer

Eliza Adebayo discovered her passion for fashion at the age of 12, sewing doll clothes from her grandmother's fabric scraps. Today, she stands as one of Nigeria's most respected bespoke designers and educators.

With a degree in Fashion Design from Polimoda and over 15 years of experience, Eliza has dressed celebrities, brides, and business executives. But her true joy lies in mentoring â€“ seeing a student cut their first pattern or a client's face when they see their finished garment.

Every piece that leaves her atelier carries her signature: impeccable fit, clean lines, and a touch of gold.

Mission

To create garments that empower women and equip aspiring designers with world-class skills, bridging the gap between tradition and contemporary luxury.

Vision

To become Africa's leading luxury atelier and fashion academy, known for excellence, innovation, and preserving the artistry of bespoke tailoring.
